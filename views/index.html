A Message From the Couple

To our dear friends and family,

We are so excited to have you join us in the celebration of our marriage this June. It was almost 10 years ago, that we locked eyes for a brief moment in a UofT cafeteria and noticed each other for the first time. According to Jason, Andrea spotted him first. According to the truth, it was him that got caught looking. While neither of us thought then that that moment would lead to a wedding and starting a life together in New York, we both agree now that we wouldn’t have it any other way.

Each and every one of you have had an impact on who we both have become through your support, guidance, friendship, and love. There’s nothing we want more than to be surrounded by our favourite people on our special day, and we hope you will be able to be there.

See you soon ☺

Love,
Andrea & Jason
